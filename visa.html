Visa Information

Paris, France

For visiting Paris, tourists need a Schengen visa. The processing time is around 10-15 days, and a fee of $60 applies.

Maldives

No prior visa application is needed for tourists staying up to 30 days.

Bali, Indonesia

Ensure your passport has at least six months of validity. Visa-free entry is available for many nationalities for a 30-day stay.
